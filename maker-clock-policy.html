Privacy Policy for MakerClock

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Precise Location: Our app requires ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION permission to scan for and connect to your Bluetooth-controlled clock devices. This data is processed locally and shared with advertising partners for app functionality.

2. Third-Party Services

Advertising and Analytics: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use Device IDs and approximate location data to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

3. Security

All data transmitted from the app is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS).

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Our app does not use a user account system. Technical data such as crash logs are used only for analytical purposes to improve app stability.
